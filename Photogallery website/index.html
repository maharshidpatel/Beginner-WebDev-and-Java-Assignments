Hello, I am Maharshi Patel, a landscape and sunset photography enthusiast based in Hamilton. With a love for capturing the beauty of nature, I have been pursuing photography as a hobby for several years.

In my free time, I can be found exploring the great outdoors, whether hiking, camping or just taking a walk in the park, always with my camera in hand, ready to capture the perfect shot. I have a keen eye for capturing the magic of nature and have a special talent for capturing the stunning colours of sunsets and the serenity of landscapes.

I have a growing collection of beautiful photographs that I love to share with my family and friends and have recently decided to take the next step and share my work with a wider audience by creating a photography webpage and gallery.

My passion for photography is not limited to just capturing the perfect shot but also learning and experimenting with different techniques and styles. I am always eager to learn and improve my skills and take my photography to the next level.

When not behind the lens, I can be found spending time with her family and friends, travelling and exploring new places, and enjoying all the wonderful things life has to offer.
