Block Dodger

Maharshi Patel

000738366

Block Dodger is a fast-paced, action-packed game where the player must navigate a small character through a field of obstacles. The objective of the game is to avoid the moving blocks for as long as possible. The blocks come in different sizes, and the player must dodge them by moving up or down using the Mouse. The game ends when the player collides with a block, and their score is based on how long they were able to survive. Block Dodger is easy to play but difficult to master, making it a fun and addictive game for all ages.
